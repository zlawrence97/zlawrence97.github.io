Implementation

At first I spent a bit of time trying to find correlation between the two images, similar to what I did in the first project to align R, G, B variations of the same photo. The same correlation function was not working on the images I had so I needed to find another way to match up similar points in both images. Of course I went down the route of SIFTing both images, Matlab does not have a method that does this but it does have a SURF method and I used this to find the 15 strongest points in each image.
Next, was to match the strongest points in both images to find overlap.
